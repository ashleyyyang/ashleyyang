About

UX designer, engineering student.


Hello, I’m Ashley ☺

I design to make the complex easily digestible, whether that means:
☛ analyzing systems to organize information on solar race car displays,
☛ creating data flow diagrams to accelerate healthcare security implementation, or
☛ remodeling an analytical dashboard to better inform business owners of their marketing performances.

When I’m not designing, you can find me drawing or taking photos while listening to the Bon Appétit Foodcast!

I’m always available to chat!
