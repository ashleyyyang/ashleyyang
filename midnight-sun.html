Midnight Sun

UX DESIGN, CASE STUDY

Leading the driver display design for the Midnight Sun XIV solar race car.

Team: Adrian and Robi (Interiors Leads), Arshan (Firmware Lead), and me!
Role: UX Project Lead
Duration: May 2019 - present

Prototype →

Midnight Sun builds a solar car from scratch every two years to compete at the American Solar Challenge (ASC). With Practicality as a multiplying factor in the 2020 ASC score, the new Interiors team was created to maximize our score and push the boundaries of consumer-centered solar vehicles.

As the UX Lead within the Interiors team, I tackled the question: How might we create the most intuitive, innovative, and safe driving experience?
